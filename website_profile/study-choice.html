study-choice

I've chosen this program because I believe it offers the best prospects for my future. Furthermore, I've always had a keen interest in computers, especially in the realm of gaming. I'm hopeful that this program will allow me to further develop these interests.

Regarding my career aspirations, I'm currently torn between pursuing a path in cybersecurity or game development. Additionally, I've contemplated a career in law enforcement within the ICT field, mainly because my father is already working as an officer in this area. With this program, I aim to acquire solid programming skills and enhance my communication abilities, both of which will be essential in my interactions with fellow ICT professionals and the general public.

On a related note, during my visit to HZ's open day, I had the pleasure of meeting Valeria, whose insights and discussions greatly piqued my interest in this program.
