Who am I?

Hi I am Lars. I am 19 years old. I turn 20 this year on the 1st of December which is kind of crazy because I don't feel that old yet and then my teens are over. I had a rather confusing school life before I got here. I started in Terneuzen at het Zeldenrust college, where I started with VWO billingiyual teaching. After two years I went to HAVO but still bilingual teaching. In my fourth year I failed the year and transferred to het Reyneart College in Hulst. Here I graduated and went on to go into a gap year. I was already working as A waiter then but because my ex worked at the same place and the salary wasn't too good I switched workplaces to go work with my best friend Joep. In this gap year I went looking for a study and that is how I ended up here at HZ.

Where am I from?

I was born in Terneuzen and I have lived most of my life here. My parents got divorced when I was twelve and after a couple of years after the divorce my mom went to live with her new husband in Hulst. I now have two houses to live at and share these with my brother, sister and in Hulst I even have a stepbrother, stepsister and halfbrother who is only 4 years old.
