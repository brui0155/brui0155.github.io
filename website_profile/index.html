My reason why I chose HBO ICT

In my gap year I did not know what I wanted to study but I needed to start next year or otherwise I would be stuck working as a waiter for the rest of my life. Not that being a waiter is a bad job but I could not see myself as a waiter my whole life. So as any person would do that has no idea what he wants to do for the rest of his or her life I set out to look at studies and what I wanted to do whilst I had my gap year. I got the tip to look into ICT so with this newly collected information I set out and looked what my options were. Keep in mind that I hadnt even considered going to this study because in high school I have always thought that I would become a teacher in history or hotel management. So then I found out there is a ICT study near where I live at a place called Middelburg. I went to the open days and I immediatley loved the place, people and how the study works. I applied and now here I am.

My previous experience with coding

My previous experience of coding or almost anything related to ICT is basically non existing however I have always liked playing games or use the internet. I have talked earlier to my parents or my stepfather that working in the development of games would be something I would love to do but I never went further then just thinking about it. Over the years of playing videogames my love for them has only grown more and more. I even bought a gaming laptop just to play more games. So as you can read I do not have much experience but I have always thought about programming and I would love to get to know how this crazy yet stunning world works.
